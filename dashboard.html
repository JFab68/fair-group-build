About This Work

The FAIR Group is a bipartisan Arizona coalition led by Representative Powell. These six working groups are developing the evidence-based reform recommendations that will form the coalition's 2027 legislative agenda. Infrastructure is provided by the Praxis Initiative, a 100% system-impacted nonprofit based in Phoenix.
